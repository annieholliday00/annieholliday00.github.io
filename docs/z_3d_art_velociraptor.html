velociraptor skull
back
I've always had a keen interest in dinosaur fossils and their structure since I was very little, and recently rediscovered this obsession while brainstorming model ideas.

This velociraptor skull was an experiment with detail, accuracy, and uv mapping, to see if I could model something that recquired intense realism.

I chose to follow a real reference of a velociraptor skull [depicted at the bottom of the page] and worked from a series of planes to get an outer shell of the skull. I then modelled inwards to build up the caverns and pillars of the skull.
