My dissertation project was a VR edutainment game about PTSD and Trauma. The gameplay includes wielding a paintgun to destroy 'intrusive thoughts', who are the wolf enemies, as well as painting the environment using the same weapon.

Heavily inspired by 'Psychonauts' and 'Okami', the intention behind the game was to teach about mental health within a character's 'mindscape', by showing symptoms as physical game mechanics and interactions between the player and other characters, with a heavy focus on art as a therapy medium. Four levels are intended to represent four aspects of PTSD.

The models for Doug and the Wolves were designed, modelled, textured, rigged, and animated by me. The low poly style was necessary for it to function properly within Virtual Reality.
