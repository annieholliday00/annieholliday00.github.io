vintage camera
back
I found a vintage camera at a friend's house and took some images on the chance that I might model it. I figured the old textures on the camera would be interesting to try and recreate.

My goal was to texture the model with my drawing tablet, which allowed me to add smaller details by hand such as the scratches in the worn leather and the dust on the lens.

Unfortunately the back of the model is unfinished because I didn't take photos of the backside of the camera, but I'm pleased with what I could finish.
